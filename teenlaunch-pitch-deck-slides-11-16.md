# TeenLaunch Pitch Deck Slides 11-16

Design direction: Design 13, clean blue theme, modern gradients, concise copy, youth-friendly but professional.

## Slide 11 - Landing Page

TeenLaunch helps students aged 10-19 discover opportunities, build entrepreneurial confidence, and grow through innovation, competitions, and personal development.

Key message:
- One platform for opportunities, competitions, resources, debate practice, and AI-guided support.
- Clear navigation built around how teenagers actually search: work opportunities, academic competitions, non-academic competitions, resources, soft skills, and chatbot help.

## Slide 12 - Opportunities

TeenLaunch makes youth opportunities easier to find and compare.

Includes:
- Startup programmes
- Leadership camps
- Innovation workshops
- Internships
- Youth entrepreneurship programmes

Core tools:
- Search and filters by category, age group, deadline, online/physical mode, and beginner/advanced level.
- Opportunity cards, recommended opportunities, and trending opportunities.

## Slide 13 - Resources

TeenLaunch supports students before they apply, compete, or present.

Includes:
- Workshops and upcoming events
- Event calendar
- Past user showcase
- Winning teams and projects
- Inspiration section
- Pitching tips and presentation advice

Outcome:
- Students learn from examples, prepare faster, and present with more confidence.

## Slide 14 - Competitions

TeenLaunch organizes competitions into clear pathways.

Academic:
- Language
- Mathematics
- Science
- Humanities

Non-academic:
- Startup pitch competitions
- Debate competitions
- Public speaking competitions
- Innovation challenges

Each listing shows:
- Event calendar, deadlines, details, rules, timeline, prize information, prize pool, and registration links.

## Slide 15 - Soft Skills & Debate

Hero message:
"Speak. Think. Lead."

TeenLaunch helps students practise communication and critical thinking.

Includes:
- Debate basics, argument building, rebuttal techniques, public speaking tips, and critical thinking exercises.
- Debate timer, practice area, debate topics, random motion generator, and AI feedback assistant.
- Debate resources, mini quizzes, speaking challenges, mock debate activities, progress tracking, and skill badges.

## Slide 16 - Product Focus

TeenLaunch is built around the original objective:
- Help teenagers discover opportunities.
- Encourage entrepreneurship and innovation.
- Build personal development skills.
- Keep the experience clear, concise, and beginner-friendly.

Why it matters:
- Teenagers often miss opportunities because information is scattered, unclear, or not age-appropriate.
- TeenLaunch turns discovery, preparation, and practice into one simple journey.
